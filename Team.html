MUNIA GANGULI

Senior Principal Scientist

I have been working in the area of nanomaterials for biological applications. A PhD in Chemistry from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore and Masters in Chemistry from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, I have built a research group at CSIR-IGIB since 2004 where me and my students have developed different nanocomplexes and nanoparticles for gene and drug delivery. One of our special focus areas is on developing new nanomaterials for cargo delivery to the skin. I have been a recipient of the National Bioscience Award for career development, Department of Biotechnology (2012) and am a SERB POWER fellow (Department of Science and Technology, 2021).

In the era of proteomics, genomics, and lipidomics, I am studying the sweet side of biology, ‘glycosaminoglycans’.
