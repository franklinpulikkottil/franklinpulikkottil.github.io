Designing the perfect vehicle!

The broad interest of our lab is to develop new methods for cargo delivery to cells and tissue using nanoparticles and nanocomplexes with important clinical implications. Our major interests are:

  • Designing and developing peptide-based nanocomplexes for gene delivery to different cells and tissues
  • Understanding the intracellular behaviour of nanocomplexes for improving gene delivery efficiency
  • Developing safe and efficient methods of macromolecule delivery to skin
  • Functionalizing different inorganic and organic nanoparticles for a wide range of biomedical applications
